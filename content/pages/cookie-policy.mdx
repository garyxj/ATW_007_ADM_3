## Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how the **AI Future Self Generator** uses cookies and similar technologies to provide a safe, family-friendly experience when generating future-career portraits for kids.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to remember preferences, improve performance, and enable certain features.

## How We Use Cookies

- **Essential cookies**: Keep you signed in, maintain session security, and deliver core functionality.
- **Performance cookies**: Help us understand usage (e.g., which steps are slow) so we can improve speed and reliability.
- **Preference cookies**: Remember language, locale, and UI settings for a smoother experience.

## Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted analytics or payment providers. These partners are contractually required to handle data securely and only for agreed purposes.

## Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect sign-in, uploads, or generation steps.

## Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy. Changes take effect when posted on this page. Check periodically for updates.
